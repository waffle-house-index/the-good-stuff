Buttermilk Biscuits

Description

Nothing beats biscuits on the morning. This is my own personal recipe developed and battle tested through many busy sunday brunch services.I hope it will serve you well for a realxed Sunday at home.

Ingredients

For this recipe you will need...

  • 4c. all purpose flour
  • 2TBL. baking powder
  • ½tsp. baking soda
  • 2tsp. kosher salt
  • 1c. cold unsalted butter(cubed)
  • 2c. cold butter-milk

Steps..

  1. Begin by pre heating ypur oven to 450 degrees farenheit, and cutting your butter into small cubes then placing them into the freezer
  2. Next whisk together your flour,baking powder,baking soda,and salt into a medium sized work bowl.
  3. After that get your butter from the freezer and add it to your dry ingredients. Using your hands work the butter into the dry mix forming pea-sized chunks of butter
  4. Put this mix into the fridge for 15 minutes to cool hte butter down
  5. Once the dry mix has had time to cool add your cold butter-milk to the bowl. Mix lightly using a rubber spatula until the dough has just began to come together then dump this out onto a clean slighty floured work surface
  6. Using your hands form the dough into a ball then lightly press that ball into a disc about ½ an inch in thickness
  7. Use a cookie ring to punch out your biscuits transfering them to a baking sheet.
  8. Place the baking hseet into the center rack of the oven allow to cook for 11 minutes
  9. Once the 11 minutes has passed pull them out of the oven and allow them to cool on the baking sheet before transfering htem to a bowl lined with a clean linen towel
  10. Finally serve and enjoy!
