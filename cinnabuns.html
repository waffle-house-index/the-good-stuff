Cinnamon rolls with cream cheese icing

Warm pillowy cinnamon rolls with cream cheese icing.Can't beat it.

*This is recipe is measured out in grams and requires the use of a kitchen scale.*

You will need...

For the dough

  • 500g. all purpose flour
  • 60g. granulated sugar
  • 10g. salt
  • 42g. unsalted butter,room temperature
  • 140ml. water
  • 140ml. whole milk
  • 7g. yeast

    • Making the dough

    1. Begin by combining your flour,sugar,and salt into a mixing bowl
    2. To this add your room temp butter. Using your hands work the butter into the dough
    3. On the stove heat up your milk and water to about 95-100 degrees farenheit
    4. add your yeast to the warm liquid, cover and loow yeast to bloom,about 10 minutes
    5. combine your liquid to your dry mix and begin to knead the dough until it becomes a smooth ball
    6. place the dough into an even larger bowl to allow it room to rise. place this dough covered into the fridge for 24 hours

For the filling

  • 125g. brown sugar
  • 10g. cinnamon

Making the filling

  1. Combine the sugar and cinnamon together
  2. Mix together until combined, set aside

For the icing

  • 85g. cream cheese, soft,room temp
  • 140g. powdered sugar
  • 42g. unsalted butter, melted
  • 2Tbl. whole milk
  1. Begin by melting the butter, set aside to cool slightly
  2. Combine the soft cream cheese with the powdered sugar
  3. Add in the melted butter and mix to combine
  4. Now add your milk
  5. whisk all of this together until smooth
  6. place this in the fridge until its time to glaze the rolls

Time to bake

24 hours have passed, your dough should have risen doubling in size.

  1. First pull the dough out of the fridge and give it about 10 minutes to warm up to room temp.
  2. Place your dough onto a lightly floured work surface and using a rolling pin begin to roll out the dough in to a circle about half an inch thick
  3. Pour your cinnamon/sugar mix on to the dough and spread almost alway the way to the edges of the dough
  4. Now its time to roll the dough into a log to form the cinnamon rolls.
  5. Cut the log into 2-3 inch rounds, place these rounds onto a baking sheet,cover lighty with a clean kitchen towel and allow to proof for 45 minutes to an hour
  6. in a preheated oven set to 375 place the cinnamon rolls on a center rack and allow ot bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown
  7. Allow 15 minutes for the rolls to cool, then glaze using hte cream cheese icing and enjoy!
